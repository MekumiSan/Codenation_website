What I do

Another point of view is a fledgling model making company utilising over twenty years of fabrication knowledge and experience in the model making industry. We aim to provide creative solutions to bespoke projects- large or small- for clients across all disciplines. In other words, we can create almost anything!

Why I do it

At a time in which VR and digital renderings are easily accessible, a model may seem obsolete. However, we believe that a high quality physical item still has its place, and can really help in selling an idea and provoking interest in a product. People are still attracted to the tactile nature of a physical scale model, and we pride ourselves on delivering high quality work that will stand the test of time.

Bespoke to fit any budget

My aim is to tailor projects to clients' needs. We are always happy to meet to discuss options, and will endeavour to offer a solution to fit any budget or vision- however large, small or ambitious.
